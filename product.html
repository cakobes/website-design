OUR PRODUCTS

Phoenix Health Consultancy ltd was set up in response to the growing aesthetic market and the demand for safe, effective medical treatment.

  • Anti-Wrinkle Injections

    Anti-wrinkle injections, work by relaxing the facial muscles that are creating wrinkles in the skin.... For a more smother, refreshed appearance as well as prevention for signs of ageing. All clients a given a full medical consultation, where a medical professional will answer all your questions. A tiny needle is used which causes slight discomfort in some clients, we do offer a local anaesthetic cream. The treatment isn’t permanent hence it is very popular with clients in comparison to surgery. As it is safer, quicker results, non permanent; people usually require top ups after several months. The most common areas injected are between the eyebrows, the forehead and the crow’s feet. In time with repeated treatments, the lines should gradually fade. We also treat other areas such as excessive sweating, teeth grinding. Each client also has a free follow up consultation. Contact us for further information
